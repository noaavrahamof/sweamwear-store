System Description

The Beach Store Analytics system provides insights into sales and inventory trends for our beach-themed retail store. We specialize in swimwear, sunglasses, hats, beach umbrellas, and other summer essentials. The system aggregates data from our point-of-sale systems and online store to track product performance, customer preferences, and seasonal trends.

Graph Significance

The embedded Tableau Public dashboard below visualizes key metrics such as sales by product category, seasonal demand fluctuations, and inventory turnover rates. This data helps our team optimize stock levels, plan promotions, and understand customer behavior. For example, spikes in swimwear sales during summer months inform our marketing strategies, while hat and sunglasses trends guide inventory restocking decisions.
